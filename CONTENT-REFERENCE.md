## TEACHING PROCESS

Building "a healthy human society and self-sufficient people" through adaptation to modern scientific and technological advancement. Develops student thinking skills, imagination, curiosity, and creativity.

### Methodology
- Curriculum Planning: "Specific academic plans" and daily lesson structures
- Technology Integration: Computers, projectors, and whiteboards
- Diverse Methods: Lectures combined with participatory learning and group discussions
- Practical Application: Regular science practicals
- Research Opportunities: Computer lab and library access
- Individualized Support: One-to-one instruction for struggling learners
- Qualified Staff: Experienced head teachers and teaching personnel

### Campus Features
- Merit-based scholarships for high-achieving students
- Campus free from smoking and political activity
- Continuous electrical power supply
- Natural, secluded campus environment
- 24/7 CCTV security monitoring
- On-campus canteen facilities

## CAMPUS RULES & REGULATIONS

### Core Rules
Students must practice truthfulness, maintain classroom discipline, and handle school property carefully. Respectful behavior toward all community members is mandatory. "Smoking inside the school premises is not permitted" (zero-tolerance). Political activities are prohibited. All institutional orders must be followed.

### Classroom Conduct
- Remain seated unless permitted by teachers
- Greet instructors appropriately
- Bring own materials
- Raise hands before speaking
- Dispose of waste properly
- Record homework daily in diaries with important notices

### Personal Appearance
- Boys: short hair
- Girls: braided hair
- Uniforms required
- Short nails
- No hair coloring
- Girls: no excessive ornaments

### Prohibited Items
Electronic devices (mobiles, MP3 players), cosmetics, and jewelry are banned from campus.

### Attendance
Students must arrive within scheduled times.

## SCHOLARSHIP PROGRAM

### Type
Siraj Mia Memorial Foundation Scholarship Program — merit-based.

### Eligibility (ALL required)
- Minimum 80% attendance in current academic year
- Class level: 5–9 only
- Good behavioral record with no disciplinary issues
- Strong academic performance in current year
- Must pass the qualifying scholarship exam

### Award
Full tuition fees for six months (July to December of the upcoming academic year). Two deserving students per eligible class.

### Qualifying Exam
- Subjects: English, Bangla, Mathematics
- Focus: Current academic year syllabus

### Application Process
1. Complete scholarship form and register for exam
2. Sit for the qualifying examination
3. Selected candidates advance to interview stage
4. Winners announced publicly
