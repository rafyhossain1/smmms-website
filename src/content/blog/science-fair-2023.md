The annual Science Fair 2023 was held on January 22, showcasing the remarkable creativity and scientific curiosity of our students. Participants presented diverse projects ranging from renewable energy solutions and water purification systems to biodiversity studies and physics demonstrations. Each project reflected months of research, experimentation, and dedication to understanding scientific principles.

The fair provided a platform for students to communicate their findings, answer questions from judges and visitors, and appreciate the work of their peers. Visitors were impressed by the innovative thinking demonstrated in projects that addressed real-world challenges. Recognition was given to outstanding projects, and winning students were celebrated for their scientific excellence. The Science Fair 2023 reinforced our belief that science education empowers students to become problem-solvers and innovators for the future.
