A memorable farewell celebration was organized for the SSC 2021 batch, marking the end of their journey at SMMM School. The event brought together students, teachers, and parents in a warm and emotional gathering filled with laughter, tears, and cherished memories. A special cake-cutting ceremony highlighted the occasion, with students and teachers coming together to celebrate the accomplishments and friendships forged over the years.

During the farewell, students shared heartfelt messages expressing gratitude to their teachers and reflecting on their experiences at school. Teachers spoke about the growth they observed in their students and the pride they felt in their achievements. Parents appreciated the school's contribution to their children's personal and academic development. The evening concluded with students exchanging contact information and promising to stay connected, recognizing that while their school life had ended, the bonds they formed would remain lifelong.
