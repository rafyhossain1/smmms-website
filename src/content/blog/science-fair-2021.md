The 2021 Science Fair, held on November 6, was a tremendous success that showcased the innovative spirit of our students. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, participants presented creative projects that ranged from environmental conservation initiatives to technological innovations. Each display demonstrated the students' commitment to scientific inquiry and their ability to think critically about real-world problems.

The fair provided an excellent platform for students to communicate their research findings and receive feedback from peers and teachers. Judging was based on originality, scientific accuracy, presentation quality, and practical application. Winners were recognized for their outstanding work, and all participants were celebrated for their curiosity and dedication. The 2021 Science Fair reinforced our belief that scientific thinking is essential for developing the next generation of innovators and problem-solvers.
