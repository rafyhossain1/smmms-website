The Siraj Mia Memorial Foundation is delighted to announce the launch of our new humanitarian initiative—the Weekly Friday Lunch Program. Beginning October 31, 2025, we have committed to providing nutritious meals to underprivileged members of our community every Friday. This program reflects our core mission to support and uplift those in need.

Our school community has been actively involved in organizing this initiative. Students and teachers volunteer each week to prepare and distribute meals, making this a meaningful opportunity for our young people to practice social responsibility. The program has already reached dozens of families in the surrounding areas, providing not just food but also dignity and hope.

We believe that hunger should never be a barrier to education or wellness. Through this program, we aim to strengthen community bonds and demonstrate that together, we can make a tangible difference in people's lives. We invite more community members and organizations to join us in this noble endeavor.
